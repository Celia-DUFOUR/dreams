Summer Dance Intensive:

The Summer Dance Intensive is an immersive training program designed for serious dancers looking to refine their skills, expand their repertoire, and experience intensive training in a supportive and inspiring environment. The intensive typically spans several weeks and includes daily classes in ballet, contemporary, jazz, conditioning, and choreography, taught by guest instructors and industry professionals. Participants also have the opportunity to collaborate on original choreographic projects and perform in a culminating showcase at the end of the program.
